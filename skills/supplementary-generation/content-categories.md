# Content Categories

Per-category guidance for supplementary generation.

## Environments / Settings
Multiple angles and perspectives for shot variety. Time-of-day variants when scenes span different moments. Atmospheric conditions (fog, rain, light quality). Wide establishing + tighter detail views.

## Props
Turnaround-style multi-angle design on neutral background. Include scale reference. Describe material properties: surface finish, reflectivity, weight, wear.

## Costumes
Full-body with fabric detail across postures. Multiple lighting conditions for color accuracy. Close-up texture for key fabrics.

## Effects / VFX
Visual effect with technical parameters: opacity, particle behavior, emission pattern. Reference real-world physics.

## Concept Art
Creative exploration -- push boundaries, try unexpected interpretations. Multiple style approaches for the same subject. Directional reference, not for direct use in generation.

## Mood Boards
Tonal references: color palettes, lighting moods, texture samples. Aligns creative direction before committing to a look.

## Product / Brand Content
Hero shots with logo integration. Brand asset compilations. Preserve exact brand colors, typography, and identity marks.
