# Project Style Keywords

Aesthetic vocabulary for creative planning and style_guide composition. Starting points -- blend, invert, or extrapolate freely.

## Filmmaker Signatures

- **Wong Kar-Wai** -- saturated film colors, neon bokeh, rain-soaked streets, step-printing blur, handheld drift, cigarette haze halation
- **Wes Anderson** -- dead-center symmetry, pastel color blocking, lateral tracking, snap zooms, precise tableaux, deadpan timing
- **Miyazaki** -- watercolor skies, gentle wind motion, warm pastoral palette, nature details, expressive gestures
- **David Fincher** -- cold desaturated, precision framing, slow dolly, razor-sharp shadows, sterile dread pacing
- **Kubrick** -- one-point perspective, symmetrical frames, slow hypnotic zooms, wide-angle distortion, ominous silence
- **Denis Villeneuve** -- monumental minimalism, foggy desaturation, vast negative space, brutalist scale, bass-droning sound
- **Ridley Scott** -- smoky volumetric shafts, practical firelight, industrial textures, layered silhouettes, rain-mist
- **PTA** -- long roaming steadicam, naturalistic tungsten, textured 35mm grain, period patina
- **Scorsese** -- swaggering tracking shots, crash-zoom emphasis, freeze-frame punctuation, kinetic crowd staging
- **Nolan** -- IMAX-scale wides, practical light realism, cool steel-blue grade, restrained handheld urgency
- **Tarantino** -- trunk POV, whip-pans, grindhouse grit, saturated primaries, needle-drop energy
- **Analog horror** -- CRT scanlines, found-footage shake, surveillance zoom, corrupted glitches
- **VHS nostalgia** -- tracking artifacts, oversaturated warmth, lo-fi grain, magnetic tape wobble
- **Y2K pop** -- high-key shine, metallic highlights, lens bloom, liquid gradients, bubblegum neon
- **Luxury editorial** -- controlled softbox, highlight roll-off, shallow DoF, minimal palette, slow-motion fabric drift
- **Surreal dread** -- uncanny still framing, oversaturated single hue, spatial impossibility, mannequin geometry

## Cinematographer Styles

- **Roger Deakins** -- precise shadow geometry, underlit atmosphere, minimal sources, single motivated key
- **Emmanuel Lubezki** -- natural light only, golden hour immersion, long fluid takes
- **Bradford Young** -- intimate ambient light, rich skin tones, underexposed warmth, velvet shadows
- **Hoyte van Hoytema** -- shallow DoF, single motivated source, IMAX naturalism
- **Robert Richardson** -- bold hard light, saturated color, theatrical contrast, dramatic flares

## Era Keywords

1970s (warm earth, tungsten halation, naturalistic handheld), 1980s (neon, hard contrast, synthwave, chrome), 1990s (desaturated cool, indie grain, muted palette), Y2K (chrome futurism, iridescent, aggressive digital), 2010s (teal-orange, digital clean, shallow DoF, lens flare)

## Style Guide Composition

A style_guide covers 5 dimensions:
1. **Visual surface** -- color palette, grade, film stock, grain, saturation, contrast
2. **Camera behavior** -- lens choice, movement vocabulary, framing tendencies
3. **Editorial rhythm** -- pacing, cut frequency, hold duration, transition style
4. **Emotional atmosphere** -- mood temperature, tension level, intimacy distance
5. **Narrative attitude** -- tone of voice, irony level, earnestness, genre awareness

## Blending

Best results come from unexpected fusions: "Deakins lighting + Y2K color", "Miyazaki palette + Fincher framing", "Wong Kar-Wai mood + Wes Anderson composition." Do not limit to listed styles -- extrapolate from any filmmaker, photographer, era, or genre.
