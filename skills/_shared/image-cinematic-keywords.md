# Image Cinematic Keywords

Technical vocabulary for image prompts. Use as inspiration -- mix, blend, and expand freely.

## Camera and Lens

**Focal lengths:** 24mm (spatial presence, slight unease), 35mm (indie intimacy, documentary), 50mm (human-eye neutral), 85mm (portrait compression, emotional distance), 135mm (voyeuristic separation, loneliness)

**Aperture:** f/1.4 (razor DoF, extreme isolation), f/2.8 (subject separation), f/5.6 (moderate depth), f/11 (deep focus, hyperfocal)

**Shot sizes:** EWS (establishing, scale), WS (full body), MWS (action + surroundings), MS (conversational), MCU (dialogue, emotion), CU (intimacy, reaction), ECU (texture, tension), Insert (object detail)

**Lens types:** anamorphic (oval bokeh, horizontal flares, prestige cinematic), spherical (clean natural), vintage glass (aberration, warmth), macro (texture revelation), tilt-shift (selective focus)

**Angles:** worm's eye (monumental), low angle (power, heroic), eye-level (neutral), high angle (vulnerability), bird's eye (omniscience), Dutch (unease, tension), OTS (conversation), POV (immersive)

**Composition:** rule of thirds, center-weighted symmetry, negative space (isolation), leading lines, foreground obstruction (voyeuristic depth), frame-within-frame, diagonal tension, depth layering

## Lighting

**Sources:** tungsten fresnel, softbox, rim light, practical lamp, window daylight, neon, candle, TV/phone glow
**Quality:** hard (crisp shadows), soft diffused (wrap-around), specular (pinpoint highlights)
**Temperature:** 2700K tungsten amber, 3200K incandescent, 5600K daylight, 6500K overcast, 8000K blue hour
**Named setups:** Rembrandt (triangle under eye), butterfly (overhead beauty), split (half-face), high-key (bright even), low-key (dramatic contrast)
**Decay path:** describe where light hits, where it fades, where it vanishes -- forces depth and contrast
**Bounce light:** indirect light colored by reflecting surface (warm off wooden floor, cool off concrete)

## Color Science

**Grading:** teal-orange cinematic, bleach bypass desaturated, cross-processed cyan-magenta, ACES filmic
**Film stocks:** Kodak 500T (rich blacks, warm halation), Portra 400 (golden skin tones), Fuji Eterna (cool muted cinema)
**Grain:** fine 35mm, heavy 16mm grit, halation glow on bright sources
**Tone curves:** S-curve lifted blacks, faded curve raised blacks, HDR extended range

## Atmosphere

**Particles:** fog (volumetric), drizzle, dust motes (Tyndall effect in light shafts), steam, smoke wisps, breath in cold, snow
**Emotion:** melancholic, energetic, oppressive, dreamy ethereal, gritty raw, nostalgic warmth, sterile clinical, intimate quiet, epic grandiose

## Materials

**Skin:** subsurface scattering, fine pores, oily specular, velvet texture
**Fabrics:** wool tweed weave, silk charmeuse sheen, aged leather patina, denim heavy twill
**Metals:** brushed aluminum grain, polished chrome mirror, oxidized copper verdigris, matte anodized
**Surfaces:** wet asphalt with oil rainbows, weathered wood grain, condensation on glass, chipped tile

## Abstract-to-Visual Translations

Convert non-visual concepts to camera language:
- "trapped" -- vertical bars, foreground obstruction, tight crop, shallow focus
- "drifting apart" -- subjects separated by object, opposite eyelines, different light pools
- "hope" -- light shift cool to warm, fill increases, posture opens, eyes lift
- "loneliness" -- large negative space, single small figure, 85mm+ compression, empty chair
- "tension" -- tight MCU, shallow DoF, hard side light, grip on object
- "time slipping" -- condensation on glass, half-burnt cigarette, melting ice, clock in soft focus

## Aesthetic Presets

Use as starting points, customize per image:
- **Naturalistic Melancholy** -- window light, muted earth, 35mm grain, overcast, quiet negative space
- **Nocturnal Loneliness** -- sodium-vapor orange, crushed shadows, wet asphalt, teal-amber split, isolated light pools
- **Intimate Handheld** -- practical lights only, imperfect framing, f/1.4, 16mm grain, warm incandescent
- **Dreamed Memory** -- overexposed halation, pastel desaturation, soft edges, lifted blacks, ethereal diffusion
- **Neon Nocturne** -- magenta-cyan LEDs, anamorphic flares, chrome highlights, wet neon puddles, aggressive contrast
- **Luxury Editorial** -- controlled softbox, highlight roll-off, minimal palette, negative space, sharp silhouettes
- **Golden Hour Intimacy** -- warm amber backlight, halation bloom, f/1.4, Portra skin tones, natural lens flare
- **Gritty Urban** -- street realism, long-lens compression, handheld wobble, tungsten yellow, heavy grain

## AI Artifact Suppression

Use "no" inline to suppress common artifacts: "no plastic texture", "no artificial sheen", "no oily skin", "no grain" (when clean digital needed). Target visible artifacts with specific terms.
